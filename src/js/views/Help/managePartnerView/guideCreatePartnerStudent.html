How to add a student in Uniwatcher CRM?

  1. Login into your account with the credentials that you received from the Uniwatcher admin team. If you are unable to log in, kindly contact the Uniwatcher team.

  2. After logging in you will see the dashboard. From the left side menu click on Add Student option.

  3. Fill out the necessary details and click on the Submit button at the bottom.

  4. The student details will be saved. You can either go to the dashboard or search for the student in Search Student to see their profile.
