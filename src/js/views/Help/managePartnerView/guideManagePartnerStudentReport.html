How to get student manage report?

  1. Login into your account with the credentials that you received from the Uniwatcher admin team. If you are unable to log in, kindly contact the Uniwatcher team.

  2. Select the Student Manager Report option from the left side menu.

  3. Select the date ranges and status according to the requirement and click on the Show button.

  4. You will be able to see all the student details based on the filters you selected. You can also export the details by clicking on the Export button.
