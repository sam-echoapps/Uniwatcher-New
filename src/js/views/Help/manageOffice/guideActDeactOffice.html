How to deactivate and activate an office?

  1. You can deactivate an office by clicking the Deactivate button in the same row. But ensure that the number of students and staff under that office is zero. If not, then you need to reassign them before deactivation.

  2. Click on Yes to confirm deactivation.

  3. Click on the Deactivated Offices tab to view and activate the offices.

  4. Click on the Activate button in the same row to activate the office.
