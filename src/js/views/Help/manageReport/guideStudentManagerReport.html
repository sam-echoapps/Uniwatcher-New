How to get Student Manager Report?

  1. Go to the left side menu, and click on the Student Manager Report option under the Student menu.

  2. There are different sets of filter options to get the report. Select the From and To date, the office, staff names and status of the leads. Once you select the required filters, click on the Show button.

  3. This will show the list of leads with respect to the filters you have selected. You can also see a summary strip of the report.

  4. To export the report in an Excel format, click on the Export button.
