How to get Missed Reminder Report?

  1. Go to the left side menu, and click the Missed Reminder Report option under the Reports menu.

  2. There are different sets of filter options to get the report. Select the From and To date, the office and course of interest. Once you select the required filters, click on the Show button. You can sort the list based on Application sent date (ASD) or Course start date (CSD).

  3. This will show the list of staff and the number of missed reminders they own. Click on the number of reminders shown corresponding to the staff.

  4. This will give you a pop with a list of reminders for that particular staff. You can also export the same by clicking on the Export button.
