How to get a Staff Application Submission Report?

  1. Go to the left side menu, and click the Staff Application Submission Report option under the Reports menu.

  2. There are different sets of filter options to get the report. Select the From and To date, the office and the staff names.You can sort the report by ASD or CSD. Once you select the required filters, click on the Show button

  3. This will show the list of staff and the application details of students under each councillor.

  4. Click on the Staff Count button at the right side.

  5. The pop-up will show you the number of students under each staff.
