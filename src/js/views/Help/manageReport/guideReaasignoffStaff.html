How to reassign a student to another staff/office?

  1. Go to the left side menu, and click the Reassign Students option under the Students menu.

  2. In each leadâ€™s row, there is a tick box at the right side. Select the boxes of the leads that you want to reassign

  3. After selecting the leads, click on the office dropdown and choose the new office you want to reassign to.

  4. Now select the corresponding staff from the other dropdown.

  5. Click on the Submit button.

  6. Click on the Yes button to confirm.

  7. You will get a success message when the reassigning is done.

  8. Students are reassigned to a new office and a new staff.
