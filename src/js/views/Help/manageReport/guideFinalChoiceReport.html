How to get Final Choice Report?

  1. Go to the left side menu, and click the Final Choice Report option under the Reports menu.

  2. There are different sets of filter options to get the report. Select the From and To date, the office, staff names and course type. Once you select the required filters, click on the Show button. You can sort the list based on Application sent date (ASD) or Course start date (CSD).

  3. This will show the list of leads with respect to the filters you have selected.

  4. To export the report in an Excel format, click on the Export button.
