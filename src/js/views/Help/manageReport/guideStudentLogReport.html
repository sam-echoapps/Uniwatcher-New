How to get Student Log Report?

  1. Go to the left side menu, and click the Student LogReport option under the Student menu.

  2. There are different sets of filter options to get the report. Select the From and To date, the office, and staff names. Once you select the required filters, click on the Show button.

  3. This will show the list of leads with respect to the filters you have selected.

  4. To export the report in an Excel format, click on the Export button.
