How to get an Applicant Report?

  1. Go to the left side menu, and click the Applicant Report option under the Reports menu.

  2. There are different sets of filter options to get the report. Select the From and To date, the office and the staff names. Once you select the required filters, click on the Show button

  3. This will show the list of staff, the number of students under those staff and their office names. Click on the number of students to see the list of students under each staff.

  4. You can see the details of students under the selected staff. You can also export it by clicking on the Export button.
