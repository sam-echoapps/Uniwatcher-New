How to add a new institution?

  1. Login into the CRM.

  2. Click on the menu bar on the left side and click on Institution List.

  3. Click on the Add New Institute button on the right side.

  4. You will get a pop-up like below where you can add the required information.

  5. Once all the information is filled, click the Submit button at the bottom.

  6. You can go to the profile of the newly created by clicking on the ID of the institute from the list.
