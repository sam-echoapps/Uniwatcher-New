How to add a student?

  1. Go to the left side menu, and click on the Add Student option under the Student menu.

  2. Give the required details of the student, select the office and a corresponding staff to manage the student and then click on the Submit button.

  3. Once you submit the student details, you will get the success.

  4. You can view the student added by going back to the dashboard’s Assigned Leads tab. Click on the Student ID to view the student’s profile.
