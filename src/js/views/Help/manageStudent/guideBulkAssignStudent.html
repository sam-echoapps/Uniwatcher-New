How to bulk assign unassigned leads to staff?

  1. Login to the Uniwatcher CRM with the given credentials.

  2. From the left side menu, select Bulk Assign Students option.

  3. Select the office in which you want to bulk assign the leads.

  4. Select the unassigned lead which you want to assign to the staff.

  5. Now select the required staff name from the councellor dropdown.

  6. Click on the Submit button.

  7. Confirm the action.

  8. Once they are assigned you wil get a success pop-up.
