How to add a student log?

  1. Go to the student's profile page, scroll down to the Student log section and click on the Add log button on the right side.

  2. Give the required information. If you want to get a reminder email on a particular date for this log, add the reminder date as well. Then click on the Submit button.

  3. You can edit the log by clicking on the Edit button in the same row.
