How to deactivate and activate a staff?

  1. Click on the Deactivate button in the same row of the staff. Make sure the students under that staff is zero. If it is not zero, reassign those students to other staff.

  2. Give the confirmation to deactivate the staff by clicking on Yes option.

  3. To activate the staff, click on the Deactivated staff tab.

  4. Click on the Activate button for the staff you want to activate.
